This guide covers everything you need to manage your team during a CERT activation — from opening the dashboard and tracking member check-ins, to creating sub-teams, assigning tasks, and responding to field reports.

1. Accessing the Dashboard

Open a browser on your laptop or tablet and go to your team's dashboard URL:

Two separate PINs: Your dashboard PIN is unique to your team and grants full administrative access. The member PIN (001789) is shared across all teams and allows field members to check in and submit reports only. Never give your dashboard PIN to field members.

Enter your PIN and click Enter Dashboard. The PIN is case-sensitive.

Use a tablet or laptop in landscape mode for the best dashboard experience. The dashboard works on phones but the layout is optimized for larger screens.
The dashboard data is stored in memory on the server. If the server restarts, all check-in data is cleared. Members will need to re-check in.

2. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard has four main sections:

SectionWhat It Shows
MembersAll checked-in members with their name, ICS role, check-in time, and current assignment
Sub-TeamsSub-teams you've created and which members are in each one
TasksAll tasks — who they're assigned to, status, and priority
ReportsICS-213 incident reports submitted by field members

The dashboard updates in real time via WebSocket — you don't need to refresh.

3. Managing Member Check-Ins

Members check in via their phone at [team].certassist.us/member.html. They appear in your Members list within seconds of checking in.

👁
Viewing members Each member card shows: name, ICS role, check-in time, current sub-team, and assigned task.
✏️
Editing a member's name Click the member's name to edit it — useful if someone checked in with a typo.
🗑
Removing a member Use the remove/delete option on a member card if they've checked out or need to be removed.

4. Creating Sub-Teams

1
Click "Create Sub-Team" Enter a name for the sub-team (e.g., "Alpha", "Medical Unit", "Search Team 1").
2
Assign members to the sub-team Click on a member card and select which sub-team to assign them to. Members can only be in one sub-team at a time.
3
Reassign or move members You can reassign a member to a different sub-team at any time from their member card.
Sub-team names appear on the member's phone automatically — they'll know their assignment within 10 seconds of you making it.

5. Creating and Assigning Tasks

1
Click "Create Task" Enter the task title, description, and priority level.
2
Assign to a member or sub-team Select who the task goes to. Members see their assigned task on their phone immediately.
3
Track task status Tasks progress through: Pending → In Progress → Completed. Members can update their own task status from their phone.
4
Edit or cancel a task Click on any task in the Tasks section to edit its details, reassign it, or cancel it.

6. Viewing and Replying to Reports

Members submit ICS-213 General Message reports from their phones. These appear in the Reports section of your dashboard.

1
Click on any report to open it The report detail shows all ICS-213 fields: Subject, From (member name + position), Incident Type, Severity, Location, and Description.
2
Send a reply Type your response in the Reply field and click Send Reply. The member sees your reply on their phone within 10 seconds.
3
Acknowledge or escalate Use the Acknowledge button to mark a report as reviewed. Use Escalate to County to forward it to the county coordination dashboard.
Report BadgeMeaning
CriticalRequires immediate attention
HighUrgent — respond soon
MediumMonitor and respond when able
LowFYI / informational

7. Escalating to County

For incidents requiring county-level coordination, escalate directly from the report detail view.

1
Open the report Click the report that needs county attention.
2
Click "Escalate to County" The report is forwarded to the County dashboard at county.certassist.us instantly. The report is flagged as escalated on your dashboard.
County Dashboard PIN: CY2027..! — Share this only with authorized county coordinators.

9. Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
Member checked in but not showingHard refresh the dashboard (Shift+Refresh). If the server restarted, the member needs to re-check in.
Dashboard shows no data after restartAll in-memory data is cleared on restart. Have all members re-check in.
WebSocket disconnected (no live updates)Refresh the page and re-enter your PIN. This reconnects the WebSocket.
Report not showing county escalationCheck that county server is running: county.certassist.us should load.
Locked out after wrong PINWait 1 hour for automatic unlock, or contact the system administrator.
