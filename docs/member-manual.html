This guide walks you through checking in with your CERT team, viewing your assignment, and submitting incident reports during an activation. You only need a smartphone or computer with a browser — no app download required.

2. Checking In

1
Enter your full name Type your first and last name in the Name field.
2
Enter the team PIN Use the PIN provided by your team leader (shown in the table above). The PIN is case-sensitive.
3
Select your ICS role Choose your assigned role from the dropdown (e.g., Field Rescuer, Medical, Search, etc.).
4
Tap Check In You'll see a confirmation and your name will appear on the team leader's dashboard instantly.
Keep your browser tab open throughout the activation. Your assignment and messages update automatically every 10 seconds — you don't need to refresh.

3. Viewing Your Assignment

After check-in, the page shows your current status. This updates automatically as your team leader makes assignments.

📋
Sub-Team Shows which sub-team you've been assigned to (e.g., "Alpha Team", "Medical Unit"). If blank, you haven't been assigned yet — check back in a minute.
✅
Current Task Your current assigned task appears here with its status (Pending, In Progress, Completed). If you have no task yet, this section will be empty.
If your assignment doesn't appear after 30 seconds, try a hard refresh (hold Shift and tap Refresh in your browser).

4. Submitting an Incident Report (ICS-213)

Use this to report an incident, hazard, resource need, or any situation requiring your team leader's attention.

1
Tap "Submit Report" button Located in the Reports section at the bottom of the page.
2
Fill in the report fields Complete the form fields as described below.
FieldWhat to Enter
SubjectBrief one-line description (e.g., "Gas leak at 123 Oak St")
Incident TypeSelect the category that best fits (Medical, Fire, Structural, etc.)
SeverityLow / Medium / High / Critical
LocationStreet address or landmark description
DescriptionFull details — what you observed, any injuries, resources needed
3
Tap Submit Your report is sent immediately to your team leader's dashboard. Your name and position are attached automatically.

5. Receiving Replies

Your team leader can reply to your reports. Replies appear directly below the report in your Reports list, shown in a green box.

Check your reports section regularly during an activation. Replies may contain updated instructions or resource confirmations.

6. Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
"Incorrect PIN" errorDouble-check the PIN — it is case-sensitive. Try typing it manually rather than copy-pasting.
Assignment not showingWait 30 seconds — the page polls every 10s. If still blank, do a hard refresh (Shift+Refresh).
Page won't loadCheck your internet connection. Try switching between WiFi and cellular data.
Accidentally checked in with wrong nameCheck in again with the correct name — notify your team leader to remove the duplicate.
Locked out after wrong PIN attemptsWait 1 hour. The system automatically unlocks. Contact your team leader if urgent.
