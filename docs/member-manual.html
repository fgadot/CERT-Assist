Member Check-In Guide

Field Member Edition
certcommand.org  ·  v1.3  ·  2026

This guide walks you through checking in with your CERT team, viewing your assignment, and submitting incident reports during an activation. You can check in two ways: using any browser on your phone or computer (no download needed), or using the CERT Command iPhone app.

2. Checking In via Web Browser

Open any browser on your phone or computer and navigate to your team's URL (table above).

1
Enter your full name Type your first and last name in the Name field.
2
Enter the member PIN Use the PIN from the table above. The PIN is case-sensitive.
3
Select your ICS role Choose your assigned role from the dropdown (e.g., Field Rescuer, Medical, Search).
4
Tap Check In You'll see a confirmation and your name will appear on the team leader's dashboard instantly.
Keep your browser tab open throughout the activation. Your assignment updates automatically every 10 seconds — do not close the tab or your session will be lost.

3. Checking In via iPhone App

The CERT Command app (installed by your organization) provides the same check-in experience with added GPS location sharing.

1
Open CERT Command and tap "Check In" On the app's main screen, tap the Check In button to open the check-in form.
2
Enter the server address Type your team's server address (ask your team leader). Example: sapphire.certcommand.org — you don't need to type https://.
3
Enter the member PIN Use the PIN from the table above (001789 for all teams).
4
Enter your name and role Type your full name, choose your ICS role, and check any equipment you have with you.
5
Tap Check In The app saves your server and PIN so you won't need to re-enter them next time.

Location Sharing (iPhone App Only)

After check-in, your My Status screen shows a Location Sharing toggle:

ModeWhat It Does
AutoSends your GPS location to the dashboard every 30 seconds. Your pin appears on the team leader's live map.
ManualLocation is not sent automatically. Tap "Share My Location Now" whenever you want to update your position.
Auto mode is recommended during an activation so your team leader can see your position on the map at all times.

4. Viewing Your Assignment

After check-in, your screen shows your current assignment. This updates automatically as your team leader makes changes — no refresh needed.

🎨
Sub-Team Badge When your team leader assigns you to a sub-team, a colored badge appears on your screen: Red Team Blue Team Green Team Orange Team
On the web portal it appears below your name. On the iPhone app it appears under your role. If blank, you haven't been assigned yet — check back in a minute.
✅
Current Task Your current assigned task appears here with title and priority. If no task yet, you'll see "No task assigned — stand by."
Assignment updates take up to 10 seconds to appear. If your assignment still doesn't show, try a hard refresh on the browser, or wait for the next auto-poll on the app.

5. Updating Your Status

After check-in your status is Available. Use the status buttons to let your team leader know your current situation. Tapping a button always asks for confirmation before applying the change. Tap an active button again to return to Available.

StatusWhen to use it
On TaskYou are actively working an assignment.
UnavailableTemporarily unable to take assignments (break, personal need, etc.).
Need HelpYou need additional members at your location.
InjuredYou have been injured and need medical attention.
The Injured status alerts your team leader immediately. Set it as soon as you are safe to do so — do not wait.

6. Submitting an Incident Report (ICS-213)

Use this to report a hazard, incident, or situation requiring your team leader's attention.

1
Tap the "Submit Report" button Located in the Reports section at the bottom of the page (web) or in the Reports tab (app).
2
Fill in the report fields
FieldWhat to Enter
SubjectBrief one-line description (e.g., "Gas leak at 123 Oak St")
Incident TypeSelect the category that best fits (Medical, Fire, Structural, etc.)
SeverityLow / Medium / High / Critical
LocationStreet address or landmark description
DescriptionFull details — what you observed, any injuries, resources needed
3
Tap Submit Your report goes to your team leader's dashboard immediately. Your name and position are attached automatically.

7. Receiving Replies

Your team leader can reply to your reports. Replies appear in a green box directly below the report in your Reports list.

Check your Reports section regularly during an activation. Replies may contain updated instructions or resource confirmations.

8. Checking Out

When your assignment is complete and you are leaving the scene, check out so your team leader knows you're no longer available.

🌐
Web browser Scroll to the bottom of the member portal and tap Check Out. Confirm when prompted.
📱
iPhone app Scroll to the bottom of the My Status screen and tap Check Out. A confirmation dialog will appear — tap "Check Out" again to confirm, or "Cancel" if you tapped it by accident.
Checking out removes you from the team leader's roster immediately. If you need to return to the scene, you can check back in again with the same process.

9. If Your Team Leader Checks You Out

A team leader can check you out remotely from the dashboard (for example, when an activation ends or you've left the scene).

🔔
Web browser An alert box will appear saying "Your Team Leader has checked you out." The page returns to the check-in screen automatically.
📱
iPhone app A notification will appear on your screen: "Your Team Leader has checked you out." Tap OK. The app returns to the check-in screen. If the app was closed when this happened, the message will appear the next time you open it.

10. Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
"Incorrect PIN" errorDouble-check the PIN — it is case-sensitive. Type it manually rather than copy-pasting.
Assignment not showingWait 30 seconds — updates poll every 10s. If still blank, do a hard refresh (Shift+Refresh on browser) or wait for the next poll on the app.
Page won't loadCheck your internet connection. Try switching between WiFi and cellular data.
Checked in with wrong nameCheck in again with the correct name and ask your team leader to remove the duplicate.
Locked out after wrong PIN attemptsWait 1 hour for automatic unlock. Contact your team leader if urgent.
App shows "Update Required"You need a newer version of the CERT Command app. Contact your team leader to receive the update.
Location not appearing on dashboardMake sure the app has permission to access Location ("While Using App" is sufficient). Check Settings → Privacy → Location Services → CERT Command.
