The Platform

One system. Field teams and EOC, connected.

CERT Command replaces paper ICS-214 forms and radio-only comms with a live iOS app for volunteers and a web dashboard for team leaders and county coordinators.

📍

Live Member Map

Every checked-in volunteer appears on a real-time map. Team leaders see the full field picture instantly — no radio check-ins needed.

📋

ICS-213 Field Reports

Volunteers submit structured incident reports (tree down, flooding, medical need, etc.) from the field. High-severity reports auto-escalate to County EOC.

✅

Task Assignments

Team leaders create and assign tasks from the dashboard. Volunteers see their assignments on the Tasks tab, update status, and add field notes.

🟢

Status Tracking

Volunteers set their own status: Available, Assigned, Unavailable, Injured, or Needs Help. Leaders see the full team status at a glance.

👥

Sub-Team System

Organize volunteers into color-coded sub-teams. Assign tasks to entire sub-teams. Members see their team assignment on the status screen.

🏛️

County EOC Integration

A separate County dashboard aggregates all team summaries. County can broadcast alerts to all teams simultaneously and receive escalated reports.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

No app store accounts for team leaders, no central server subscription. Each team runs their own isolated instance.

1

Team Leader Starts the Server

A lightweight server runs on any VPS or local machine. Takes under 5 minutes to deploy with Docker.

2

Volunteers Download the App

CERT Command is a free iOS app. Volunteers enter the team server address and PIN — no account creation required.

3

Check In at Activation

Each volunteer checks in with their name, role, and available equipment. They appear on the dashboard map immediately.

4

Coordinate in Real Time

Leaders assign tasks, track locations, receive field reports, and manage sub-teams — all from a browser on any device.

Who It's For

Built for every level of CERT operations.

Whether you're a small neighborhood team or a county program managing multiple teams, CERT Command scales to fit.

CERT Volunteers

A simple iOS app to check in, update your status, submit field reports, and view assigned tasks. No training required — designed to work under stress.

Team Leaders

A full web dashboard to see your entire team on a live map, assign tasks, manage sub-teams, and receive ICS-compliant field reports in real time.

County EOC

A county-level dashboard that aggregates all team summaries, receives escalated high-priority reports, and broadcasts alerts to every active team simultaneously.
